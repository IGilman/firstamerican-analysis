ChatGPT Launch (Nov 2022)
The Five-Year Silence: From Q1 2020 through Q4 2024 — 20 consecutive quarters — First American made zero explicit AI or machine learning mentions in earnings calls, even while actively building what it would later call its "AI-native" platforms Sequoia and Endpoint. The company's technology narrative was framed entirely through "title automation" and "data leadership." That changed dramatically in Q3 2025, when CEO Ken DeGiorgio stated: "Both Endpoint and Sequoia, they leverage AI at the core… Both those are AI-native platforms now… we've got ChatGPT Enterprise for all 19,000 of our employees."
